Once upon a time, my computer architecture professor said
that it's fortunate that least recently used (LRU) is an
optimal cache replacement policy,
because it is simple to implement.
That never really sat right with me.
Half a decade later, I read Dan Luu's post [Caches: LRU v. random](https://danluu.com/2choices-eviction/)
and finally feel vindicated.
